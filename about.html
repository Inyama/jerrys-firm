Manta Offshore Ltd is a Nigerian marine services provider with a strong and successful record of Vessel Operations, Manning and Crewing Services, Project Management, Technical and Logistical support to major clients within the offshore oil and gas industry.

We are renowned for the quality and reliability of our vessels and equipment, our fastidious approach to safety and our unwavering commitment to the delivery of success for our clients, partners and shareholders are our core principles.

We have the necessary expertise to plan and deliver services and projects of all sizes – from short-term ‘spot hires’ of vessels, equipment or specialist personnel, through to large scale project management comprising multiple stakeholders and long-term, multi-phase operational delivery.

In addition to the delivery of our own services and products, Manta Offshore Limited is well-placed to advise and support third party companies seeking to broaden the scale or range of their own activities, or to enter the flourishing oil and gas sector here in Nigeria.
